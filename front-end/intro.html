Our Mission

When a large company wants to expand, it calls upon a vast array of talent and computation. But small businesses don’t have the luxury of spending millions on target market selection. That’s why we created an open-source, freely available web app that delivers high-quality, data-driven recommendations for where to grow.

Using the Product

It's simple! There's only two steps. First, enter a description of your company; for example, where your company is located, and what sort of customers you expect Then, rank some common factors pertaining to the company based on how important they are to you. That's all there is! We'll give you a list of the best places for your new location.

Start

How It Works

We base our recommendations on a dataset with over 400 pieces of demographic information about every region of Delaware. The dataset is analyzed using machine-learning techniques such as locality-sensitive hashing and singular value decomposition, and the description is parsed using natural-languate processing.
